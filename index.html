Read this before interpreting

A useful signal, with firm limits

01

Abstracts are incomplete

Many papers report results only in the full text or tables, so this search will miss them.

02

Not a formal p-curve

The tool cannot reliably distinguish primary hypothesis tests from secondary, adjusted or descriptive analyses.

03

Patterns are not proof

A concentration near 0.05 can have several causes. It does not establish publication bias or misconduct by any study.

04

Fields report differently

Reporting conventions, study designs and typical statistical power vary substantially between topics.

The app searches PubMed using NCBI E-utilities, retrieves up to 200 abstracts, and recognises common forms such as p = .03, p<0.05 and P ≥ 0.10. Searches are cached in your browser for seven days.
